Breakfast

The perfect egg scramble

A very easy breakfast

Save time and calories by eating this complete and nutritious meal

Ingredients
  • Eggs
  • Dairy - milk or cream
  • Fat - Butter or Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Hot Sauce
  • Fruit to accompany
Steps
  1. Crack eggs into a bowl
  2. Whisk the eggs until smooth
  3. Add a splash of dairy
  4. Heat up stove with the fat on medium in a Pan
  5. When hot, add egg mixture to pan
  6. Move eggs around until the mixture becomes completely solid
  7. Add Hot Sauce, Salt and Pepper to the Eggs and add fruit to complete the meal
