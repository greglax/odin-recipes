Lunch

The perfect avocado toast

A very easy lunch

Save time and calories by eating this complete and nutritious meal

Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Avocado
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Lemon
  • Butter
Steps
  1. Toast the bread
  2. Butter when hot
  3. Slice cheese and add to buttered bread
  4. Slice the avocado and add on top of cheese
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. Sprits a little lemon juice on
