Dinner

The perfect pasta

A very easy dinner

Save time and calories by eating this complete and nutritious meal

Ingredients
  • Pasta
  • Grape Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Parmesan
Steps
  1. Boil pasta until cooked
  2. In a bowl add diced tomatoes, crushed garlci and sliced basil
  3. Add bowl to the cooked pasta
  4. Serve with Parmesan
